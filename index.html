Frequently Asked Questions

What is your car rental process?

Reserve online or by phone, choose vehicle and schedule, provide driver details (if any), pay a deposit or full amount as per the package, and pick up or receive the vehicle. We confirm all bookings by SMS/WhatsApp.

What documents do I need?

For rentals with a driver: a valid ID (Aadhaar/Passport/Driving Licence) and contact details. For self-drive (if available): original driving licence, ID proof, and a refundable security deposit as required.

Are there any extra charges for late returns?

Yes — late returns are charged as per the tariff plan (hourly or daily rates). We recommend contacting us as soon as you know you'll be late to possibly arrange an extension and avoid higher fees.

Can I cancel my booking?

Cancellations are allowed; cancellation fees depend on the timing and the package booked. Please check your booking confirmation or contact customer support for the exact cancellation policy.

Is there a mileage limit?

Some packages include a fixed mileage allowance (e.g., 40 kms for 4 hours). Extra kilometres are charged at our per-km rate. Please check the selected tariff for precise limits.

Do you provide roadside assistance?

Yes — we provide roadside assistance for mechanical issues and breakdowns. Contact our support number and we'll assist or arrange a replacement vehicle if necessary.

How do I extend my rental period?

Contact us before your current booking ends. If a vehicle is available, we'll extend the booking and provide the updated charges. Payment for the extension can be taken online or on delivery.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept UPI, major credit/debit cards, netbanking, and cash payments depending on the booking type. Online payments are preferred for faster confirmation.

Can I rent a car without a driver?

Self-drive options depend on vehicle availability and local regulations. Please contact us to confirm self-drive availability and our requirements (licence, deposit, etc.).

Is insurance included in the rental?

Basic insurance is included as per government regulations. Additional coverages or waivers (if offered) may be available at extra cost; check your booking details for coverage limits.
